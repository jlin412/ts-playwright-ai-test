## Route architecture

Each "tab" of the stepper is a **separate URL route**. The pill UI is mostly visual; you can't navigate by clicking pills until prerequisites are met. Real navigation uses the labelled progression buttons.

| Step | URL route | Advance trigger | Status indicator |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1. Tickets | `/checkout/showing/{slug}/{showtime-id}` | `NEXT: FOOD & DRINK` | active when entered |
| 2. Food & Drink | `/checkout/items` | `NEXT: CART` | вњ” on Tickets |
| 3. Cart | `/checkout/cart` | `NEXT: PAYMENT` | вњ” on Tickets + Food & Drink |
| 4. Payment | `/checkout/payment` | `PAY $X.XX WITH CARD` *(do not click in tests)* | вњ” on all prior tabs |

The `/checkout/items` URL (concessions) is the same one we discovered earlier in the standalone recon вЂ” it's the Food & Drink step, accessible directly OR inside the ticketing flow.

## Stable selectors for K Phase 2

- Tickets URL pattern: `/\/checkout\/showing\/[^/]+\/\d+/`
- Food URL: `/checkout/items` (exact)
- Cart URL: `/checkout/cart` (exact)
- Payment URL: `/checkout/payment` (exact)
- Progression buttons: `getByRole('button', { name: /^next: food & drink$/i })`, `/^next: cart$/i`, `/^next: payment$/i`
- Ticket-type ADD: scope by the section heading "Tickets" then `getByRole('button', { name: /^add$/i })` вЂ” but be aware Day Pass also has an ADD button just above. Pick the first ADD inside the "Tickets" subsection.
- Cart summary: `getByText(/\d+ tickets?\s*В·\s*\$\d+\.\d{2}/)` вЂ” matches "1 ticket В· $34.95" / "2 tickets В· $69.90"
- Promo input: `getByLabel(/add gift card, voucher, promo code/i)` (works on all 4 tabs)
